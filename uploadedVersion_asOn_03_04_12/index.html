Thoughts form, Ideas breathe and Art expresses. Expressions choose and take many forms.
We chose natural slate, glass and power efficient subtle LED Lights.

The Bimba International Range of products designed from the natural elements leave minimal carbon footprint and makes sure that each product preserves in itself the intricacies of the nature's own expressions. A chip, a fossil, a corrosion that one finds as he picks up a wet stone from the banks of a calming lake on a warm, young day, playfully runs his finger over the straitions of a sea shell just to feel the true touch of nature, so close to oneself. Bimba International preserves that very piece of nature in each of its product. Thus the product you choose is hand-crafted, time tested, one-of-its kind in the whole world and is specially made only for You.
